Why Star Schema?

  • Optimised for analytics — straightforward aggregations and joins
  • BigQuery-friendly — columnar storage loves denormalised patterns
  • Analyst-accessible — no complex multi-table joins required

Example Query

SELECT d.category, DATE_TRUNC(date, MONTH),
  SUM(f.payment_value) AS revenue
FROM Fact_Orders f
JOIN Dim_Products d USING(product_id)
GROUP BY 1, 2

04

ELT Pipeline & CDC

Push-down processing with change data capture at every layer

Why ELT over ETL?

  • Push-down processing — leverage BigQuery's compute
  • Raw data preserved — Bronze layer keeps full audit trail
  • Flexible transforms — evolve logic without re-ingesting

CDC Approach

  • dbt incremental materialisation with unique_key
  • MERGE strategy: INSERT new, UPDATE changed
  • Processing proportional to changes, not total volume

10

Q&A — Anticipated Questions

Prepared talking points for the panel

"Why GCS + BigQuery?"
Native integration, serverless scaling, columnar storage with on-demand pricing. No cluster management.
"Why Meltano over Airbyte?"
Lightweight, built-in state management for incremental loads. Plugin architecture. Airbyte is heavier to self-host.
"Why dbt for transforms?"
SQL-first, version-controlled, built-in testing. Industry standard for ELT. Incremental MERGE for CDC.
"Why Dagster over Airflow?"
Asset-based (data-aware) vs. task-based. Native sensor support. Better DX with type checking and testability.
"Why Gradio over Streamlit?"
Minimal boilerplate, easy multi-tab merge for 6 developers in parallel. Built-in sharing.
"How does this scale?"
BigQuery scales to petabytes. GCS unlimited storage. Pub/Sub handles millions of msg/sec. dbt incremental keeps costs proportional.
